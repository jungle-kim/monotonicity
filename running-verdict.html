Running verdict: has AI made me more productive?

March 2026

Running verdict on whether AI has indeed made me more productive

The Verdict

No.

Creating some outputs have been faster, but I spend way too much time on the LLM chatbot. Context: by nature of team design and also program design, there are not necessarily more tasks that I can take upon, either from colleagues or unassigned ones.

Top existing tasks with productivity increase due to AI use.

  • Writing reports and email snippets on projects that fit industry lingo.
  • Research technical aspects of a new topic like ghg calculations of sustainable aviation fuel
  • Structuring project concept and writing terms of references for consultants who would work on it
  • Improving Excel formulas

Top new tasks enabled with AI use.

  • Creating new team tools, such as project management progress tracking.
  • Creating new personal tools, such as PII-anonymizer
  • Creating AI chatbot prompt instructions for clients on how they can use AI for documents I require.
