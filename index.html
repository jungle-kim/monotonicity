Monotonicity

In Economics, the concept of Monotonicity is a base assumption, which in its simplest form means more is better. Using AI at work allows me to do more, which makes working with AI a better way of working. This blog is where I document my attempt at it.

Navigation

In Stuff I Made I document the things I built with AI assistance: larger vibe coding projects and quick wins that simplify work. For now, they are a lot of HTML stuff.

I reflect on the topic AI at work in my blog, primarily my own attempts. I focus on an individual or team perspective, less so on the macro company or societal level. Starting off with how I think about using AI at work. I have definitely made my work easier and certainly had fun creating new tools, but I have a lingering doubt whether there was a better way to do all this.

About

I work at a publicly owned development finance institution, working on grant based technical assistance programs. The heavily bureaucratic nature of my workplace defines how I use AI, but it does help me hone in on my creativity. Along with AI, I think a lot about dramatically increasing grant programs from donors to flow to the private sector via financing institutions, including impact investors.
